Unsupervised Clustering of Seismic Signals Using Deep Convolutional Autoencoders

Author: grosphi Date: 十月 15, 2025  18:30:00

Method

无监督学习（clustering）

network: an undercomplete fully convolutional autoencoder

clustering layer: K-means

Loss function:
$$
L=(1-\lambda)L_r + \lambda L_c
$$

  1. 先把λ设为0（不考虑聚类），预训练（也算是一种自监督方法）
  2. the learned features are used to initialize the cluster centroids ($u_j$) in the feature space using k-means.
  3. fine-tuning stage，调整λ，训练分类模型

Results

DISCRIMINATING LOCAL FROM TELESEISMIC SEISMOGRAMS

训练为输入时频谱： In our previous study [21], we obtained a better result for discriminating earthquake depth between different seismic signals working in the time–frequency domain.

下图为聚类空间中的划分，从a到f，区分越来明显，clustering acc分别为50%，85%，87%，—，—，98.41%

另外，数据量仅9.2k vertical component，相比于 Meier et al. [9] trained a random forest classifier using a set of hand-selected features extracted from ~237k samples and were able to classify 95% of teleseismic records correctly.

APPLICATION FOR FIRST-MOTION POLARITY DETERMINATION

输入：We only used records with picked P-arrival time and assigned upward and downward first-motion polarity as determined by analysts. We then used 32 samples (0.32 s) around the P-arrival as the input to our deep clustering neural network (Fig. 2).

结果：不如其他监督方法。We obtained an overall accuracy of 0.97. The precision, recall, and F-score for upward polarities are 0.99, 0.82, and 0.9, respectively, although the corresponding values for the downward polarities are 0.72, 0.99, and 0.83.

由于所提出的网络架构的功能是自动特征提取和降维，我们发现较小的维度会导致更高的重构误差，从而降低聚类精度。网络维度的选择取决于问题的复杂性。遗憾的是，对于选择瓶颈维度并没有经验法则。一种经验方法是从一个过完备的自编码器开始，在预训练步骤中逐渐降低瓶颈维度，直到重构损失开始显著增加。
